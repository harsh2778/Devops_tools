Operation Sindoor

India's Strategic Response to Terrorism

Background

On April 22, 2025, a terrorist attack in Pahalgam, Jammu and Kashmir, claimed the lives of 26 civilians. The attack was attributed to The Resistance Front (TRF), an offshoot of the Pakistan-backed Lashkar-e-Taiba. In response, India launched Operation Sindoor on May 7, 2025, targeting terrorist infrastructure in Pakistan and Pakistan-occupied Jammu and Kashmir.
